Every month, we hand out fee 'cards' that display the amount required to continue tuition for the next month.
Students are required to hand in these cards back together with the amount required before the end of the month that they received the card.

You can also pay your child's tuition fees by going to our tuition centre and requesting to pay according to your child's NRIC number
There, you can also pay for your child's tuition fees in advance.

Students are unable to have private 1-on-1 lessons with our teachers as we have limited resources to handle them.

However, Students that are unable to perform well are able to sign up for additional lessons an extra day on Sunday every week for free.
Students that missed classes are able to replace them up to three times a month.

On average, each class has 15 students.
Extra classes however only has 3-5 students on average.

Our classes can however have a maximum of 20 students.

On weekdays, we typically start classes from 10am,
with the last class ending at 9pm.

On weekends, we do not have any lessons for students
as we will be doing administration duties.

All of our teachers at our tuition centres are graduates from the best schools from all over the world.

Additionally, our teachers are enrolled in a multifaceted training programme that includes modules
on pedagogy, content mastery, classroom delivery, relationship management and marking before they enter the classroom.

When a lesson is cancelled because of a public holiday, It is considered that the lesson was missed.
This means it can be replaced up to 3 times a month.
