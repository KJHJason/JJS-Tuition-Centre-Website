Programmes Offered (Secondary N/O levels)

Science

For Chemistry in both "O" science and Pure Science, every 3 lessons would be conducted in the tuition centers very own chemistry lab to help students practice more with chemistry equipment and catch up with chemistry practical lessons. Our labs are kept up to standard with the ever changing syllabus and also feature chemicals that can only otherwise be found in schools.

 

For both "O" physics and Pure Physics, the timing will also be identical to chemistry, with practical lessons every 3 lessons. Our physics labs are fitted with the latest equipment and are regularly replaced to ensure safety in equipment and reliability in tests. The teacher guiding this experiment is also a well known teacher from Raffles Institution, Mr James Charles who has over 20 years of experience in teaching physics.

 

As for the other two sciences, Biology lab sessions are also held every 3 lessons. Our Biology labs are equipped with one of the most powerful microscopes, with up to 1280 times zoom and able to produce clear images, helping students to better see and study the world of micro-organisms like bacteria and fungi. Our biology lab lessons will be taught by Mdm Melissa Lee, a teacher from Chung Cheng high school with over 35 years of expereince in teaching biology.

 

To answer further inquiries, all our physics, chemistry and biology are certified by Cambridge to conduct experiments that are similar to the ones done in the national exams. Our students are also heavily prioritised and we also have supervisors to ensure that our students are all wearing the correct protective equipment at all times, as well as follow the safety precautions

Outdoor Exploration!

For History, follow our teachers on a wild journey on the end of october to the National Museum of Singapore, where we can discover the historical relics from singapore's history and uncover the rich politics that lie during the growth of singapore form a third world country to a first world country, where we can witness rare tapes of iconic speeches performed by Mr Lee Kuan Yew and Sir David Marshall, which are only a few examples of the many things to be explored on this journey.

 

On the 25th of August, there will be a quick tour around the city to visit the most iconic places in Singapore like Little India, Kampong Glam and Chinatown to discover the cultures that are present in those areas. We bring our students to these places for them to discover the deep heritage and cultures of the different ethnic groups in Singapore.

 

Our outdoor sessions usually last between 4-5 hours and these journeys are always optional, which means that you can always opt out of these learning journeys anytime. Meals during this journey are all provided and no additional charges are required for these trips.
